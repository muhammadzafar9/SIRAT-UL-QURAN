Every Muslim has an obligation to seek knowledge.

Every Muslim has a duty to seek knowledge, as it is a fundamental aspect of their faith. This pursuit enriches their understanding of the world and strengthens their relationship with Allah. By acquiring knowledge in various fields, Muslims fulfill their obligation to contribute positively to society and lead a life aligned with Islamic principles.
